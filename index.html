Who We Are

Travel With Purpose is a 501(c)(3) with a mission to inspire others to travel with purpose and leave footprints of hope across the globe. Our focus is to empower women and girls by generating and funding projects that offer sustainable solutions financially and emotionally supporting the beneficiaries.

We aim to partner with NGOs that empower women through employment and ones that rescue, restore, educate, re-integrate, and shelter women and girls victims of sex trafficking, oppression, gender violence, and forced labor.

Loren Medina

Loren Medina is an entrepreneur, philanthropist, and avid globetrotter. She graduated from Boston University with a B.A. in Psychology and attained a Master’s in Communication Management from USC. In September of 2014, after watching the documentary & reading the book “Half the Sky", she got inspired and started a campaign to stop sexual trafficking. After raising over $10k USD she carried out her first volunteering project in Cambodia & the Philippines.

Loren’s passion is travel, with a track record of 38 countries and 6 continents. Her vision was to fuse her passion of traveling with philanthropy, which led to the birth of Travel With Purpose. Currently, Loren is working relentlessly to empower young women and bring hope into their lives through her non-profit.

Travel With Purpose + The Syria Fund

Travel With Purpose will be partnering with the 501c3 The Syria fund located in Jordan that focuses on educating Syrian children, providing humanitarian aid and creating opportunities for refugees to work and make a living. There are an estimated 1.3 million Syrians living in Jordan today which constitutes 13% of the population. More than 80% of Syrian refugees live outside of refugee camps in hosting communities in Jordan which is the population The Syrian fund works with. We will be helping The Syria Fund move their new academic program called Middle East Children’s Institute forward by raising funds to pay for the salaries of 3 Syrian women which will be hired to become outreach counselors. They will each be in charge of 150 children and responsible for reaching out to students to enroll in the program and follow up with their families. Counselors salaries are $300 USD for a year which amounts to $10,800. Our goal is to raise $15,000 USD. With the remaining funds, we will buy school supplies, hygiene kits, and emergency food clothing supplies. Donate. Be part of the journey!
